Kerry's Kitchen

Restorant

Restorant Story

Kerry's Kitchen serves well-cooked Asian and International cuisine for buffet breakfast, lunch and dinner. There are also a variety of à la carte dishes on offer.

Suited for both adults and children, the buffet lunch and dinner available on weekdays and weekends allow adults to enjoy unlimited servings of appetizers, main courses and desserts, accompanied by unlimited champagne, wines, draft beer, juices and soft drinks, while the little ones get to experience the thrill and fun of Adventure Zone.

Showcasing several live-cooking stations, passionate kitchen theatrics is matched in spirit by a team of energetic service staff at Kerry’s Kitchen. Design elements of Izumi stones, wood and clay creates a space that offers guests the freedom of just being; relaxed, comfortable and feeling right at home, with flavors and aromas transporting them back to their childhood days.
